Dungeon you can enter by talking to a foreign wanderer and revisiting Golden City after clearing the main story.<br/>It's a direct continuation of Table Mountain, so you're expected to bring items and the dungeon begins on 31F.

There's a single room Monster House featuring a Tainted Soldier every 10 floors, and a final boss on 99F.<br/>Once you defeat the final boss, fixed Monster Houses and boss monsters will no longer be generated.<br/>â€» The final boss drops a Kabra's Blade as a one-time reward.
