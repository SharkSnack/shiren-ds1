Table Mountain (also known as Kobami Valley) is the main story dungeon of Shiren 1.<br/>The dungeon is split into a number of areas, and there are overworld rest spots along the way.<br/>Backtracking is possible, but you can only go forward once you go past Cryptic Rock Valley.

This page mostly assumes you're attempting to clear the main story in 1 adventure from a new save file.<br/>If you're new to Shiren, or aren't up for the challenge, it's best to use backtracking to stock up on items<br/>and upgrade equipment at village blacksmiths to slowly work your way up instead.
